Has your site been designated for development?

Help with designated site

A designated site is an area that has been recognised for it's quality and policies have been put in place to protect it.

Examples of designated areas include national parks and the Broads, Areas of Outstanding Natural Beauty, conservation areas and World Heritage sites.
